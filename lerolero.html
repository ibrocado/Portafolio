LERO LERO (2021)

Designing a brand identity holds big significance for a designer. Here, the task was to shape a Mezcal brand's essence through its name and bottle. Lero Lero is a saying in spanish and is usually used by children as a joke. Focusing on wonder and humor, the brand revives the forgotten playfulness of adulthood, with Mezcal acting as a reminder.
