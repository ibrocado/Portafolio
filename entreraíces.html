ENTRE RAÍCES (2022)

This project resulted from an exercise evolving into a real game. Its main aim was to normalize collective, diverse imagination, offering a glimpse into a future that prioritizes the common good. We draw inspiration from the Earth and those who understood its essence before us. This was a collaborative effort with Roberta Schroeder, Ana Berrón and Renata Flores.
