AMBULANTE (2021)

The documentary tour takes place once a year in Mexico. In this case we took the concepts of community, relationship, territory and adaptation to make an identity proposal for Ambulante 2021 and its corresponding website. The meticulous use of typography was crucial for optimal functionality. This was a collaborative project with Roberta Schroeder and Lucía Galindo.
