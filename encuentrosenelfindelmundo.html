ENCOUNTERS AT THE END OF THE WORLD (2020)

The aim was to curate an archive of images and texts to then create a visual narrative tied to a title and an author. Inspired by Aldous Huxley's reflections on technology's influence and Encounters at the End of the World tackling global warming, it's centered on the pandemic theme. Amidst chaos, the narrative envisions a hopeful future emerging from a worldwide disorder.
