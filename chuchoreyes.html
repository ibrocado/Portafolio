CHUCHO REYES. LA FIESTA DEL COLOR (2022)

The project aimed to create a booklet proposal for the Chucho Reyes exhibition at Palacio de Bellas Artes. Celebrated for his vibrant and dynamic paintings, the Mexican artist was an inspiration to fellow creators and maintained a strong connection with Luis Barragán. His artworks burst with lively hues and motion, a quality this proposal strives to capture, honoring his artistic legacy that continues to resonate.
