CONTENERNOS (2023)

Recently, my awareness of feminism grew, revealing how Mexican women can adopt macho behaviors too. Focusing on mother-daughter relationships within my context, I aimed to improve how we relate to each other. Recognizing the lack of spaces for meaningful talks, I devised a booklet featuring 10 tools to take into account when talking and questions that would help create that space for safe dialogue.
