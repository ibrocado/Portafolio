CAO (2022)

The outcome of this project was a girls magazine, drawing inspiration from what I would have cherished during my childhood. As a child, was always very curious, always wanted to do handcrafts and be close to the creative world. That's why I wanted to put all this in one publication, with lots of activities for girls who want to see the world in a different way.
